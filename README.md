# digital_pet_app

Your very own digital pet. Feed it, Play with it, Let it Rest.

# Required Features  
- Dynamic Moods: Pet color and emojis update based on happiness (Green is Happy, Yellow is Neutral, Red is Unhappy).
- Pet Naming: Custom text input and button to set the pet's name.
- Auto Hunger: A timer increases the hunger level every 30 seconds automatically.
- Win: Keep happiness level above 80 for 3 minutes and you win the game.
- Loss: If Hunger level is at 100 and Happiness level is less than 10, you lose.

# Advanced Features
- Energy Level decreases during play and increases during rest.
- Visual Energy Bar: A LinearProgressIndicator that shows the percentage of energy available from 0 to 100%.

# Gameplay
- Play: Increases happiness, but consumes energy and increases hunger.
- Feed: Lowers hunger and provides a small energy boost.
- Rest: Restores energy but increases hunger over time.
